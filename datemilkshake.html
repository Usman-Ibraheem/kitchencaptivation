Date MilkShake


INGREDIENTS

• 10 dates
• 2 cups of powdered milk
• ¼ tsp of cinnamon powder
• 2 tsp of sugar
• Ice cubes

METHOD

Step 1: First of all, put 10 seedless dates in a blender.

Step 2: add 2 cups of powdered milk, ¼ tsp of cinnamon powder and 2 tsp of sugar.

Step 3: blend until smooth.

Step 4: Then add your ice cubes AND ENJOY!
