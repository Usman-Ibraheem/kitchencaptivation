GOAT MEAT SOUP

GOAT MEAT SOUP

March 10th, 2022

As an added bonus, it is great for your diet because it is lean and have less saturated fat.Goat meat is a great source of nutrients, including protein, iron, vitamin B12, zinc, and potassium.

INGREDIENTS

• Washed goat meat
• 1 chopped onion
• 4 crushed garlic cloves
• 4-6 chopped scotch bonnet
• 1 Tbsp Pepper soup spice
• 2 Tbsp black pepper
• 2 small bell peppers
• 1 sprig of fresh parsley and basil
• 3 stock cubes
• Salt to taste

METHOD

Step 1: Place the Goat meat in a pot filled with water enough to cover the meat.

Step 2: Add the onions, garlic cloves, black pepper, stock cubes and some salt and allow to come to a boil. When they begin to boil then add the remaining ingredients except the bell peppers and parsley.

Step 3: Let it cook till the meat is tender and cooked through, then lower heat to a simmer.

Step 4: Add the chopped bell peppers and the parsley and allow simmer for a minute, then turn off heat and Enjoy!
